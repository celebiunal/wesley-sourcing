About Wesley Sourcing

A European China Sourcing Partner Based in Yiwu

Wesley Sourcing is a European-oriented China sourcing partner based in Yiwu. We act as an extension of your team in China — reducing the distance between European expectations and Chinese manufacturing reality, so businesses can start small, develop confidently, and scale their sourcing over time.

Meet Wesley Neckebroeck

Wesley Neckebroeck is a Belgian entrepreneur based in Yiwu, China, and the founder of Wesley Sourcing. He works directly at the source — with suppliers, factories, quality control, and export — while bringing a European understanding of how his clients think, communicate, and run their businesses. That combination is the foundation of Wesley Sourcing: European business understanding paired with China-based execution.

From Europe to China

Wesley's approach is grounded in a European business perspective. He understands the challenges businesses face when sourcing internationally — from clarifying specifications and managing communication across cultures, to the practical realities of starting, testing, and growing a product line. This perspective shapes how Wesley Sourcing communicates, sets expectations, and supports clients from Belgium, France, the Netherlands, Germany, and across Europe.

A Long-Term Connection With China's Supply Chain

Wesley's expertise developed over time — through European business contacts, international projects, and practical, hands-on involvement with Chinese suppliers and manufacturing. His connection to China's sourcing ecosystem is further strengthened by his wife, who is Chinese and has more than 15 years of experience in the sourcing and trading industry. Her experience is a complementary strength: together they combine European business understanding with deep, first-hand Chinese sourcing experience.

Why Wesley Moved to Yiwu

Wesley chose to base himself in Yiwu — one of the world's largest wholesale markets — to be close to the source. Physical presence in China means he can communicate directly with suppliers, visit factories, follow production as it happens, carry out quality control before shipment, and coordinate consolidation and export. For clients, that local presence turns a long-distance, high-risk process into something managed on the ground by someone who understands both sides.

An Extension of Your Team in China

Most European businesses don't just need a factory contact — they need someone on the ground who works as part of their team. Wesley Sourcing understands your requirements, communicates with suppliers on your behalf, resolves misunderstandings before they become problems, follows production, checks quality, and coordinates the local details that are hard to manage from thousands of kilometres away. The real value is reducing the distance between what a European business expects and how Chinese manufacturing actually works.

Supporting Businesses From First Product Idea to Growth

Many successful businesses begin the same way: a first product, a small order, a test of the market. Wesley Sourcing is built to support that journey — and everything after it. We help companies start with realistic quantities, improve and refine their products, find suitable suppliers, and grow their sourcing operations over time. Our experience is especially valuable for SMEs and entrepreneurs, but we are not limited to them: as projects and companies grow, we support larger orders and larger sourcing operations too. We grow together with our clients — from first ideas to larger sourcing projects.

Customized Products and Flexible Sourcing

Wesley Sourcing specializes in customized and flexible sourcing: product development, private label projects, adapting existing products, prototypes and testing orders, and smaller production runs that can scale up. The ability to customize a product and start with a realistic quantity — then grow — is often what makes a project possible for a developing business.

Supplier Relationships Built Through Experience

The real value is not a large supplier database. It is supplier relationships built through years of practical sourcing experience. Across different product categories, Wesley Sourcing matches each project with suitable, communicative, and reliable suppliers — because knowing which supplier fits a specific product and business matters far more than simply collecting the most quotes.

Quality and Trust

Trust comes from process. Wesley Sourcing helps verify and select suitable suppliers, clarifies requirements to reduce communication problems, follows production, and checks quality before goods leave China. We believe the lowest price is not always the lowest cost: a reliable supplier relationship, correct quality, and smooth communication create long-term value — and help clients avoid choosing suppliers on the lowest quotation alone. Our focus is the right sourcing solution: the right supplier, the right quality, and the right long-term partnership.

Whether you're developing a first product, growing your range, or scaling established sourcing operations — think of us as your team in China. Let's discuss your project.
